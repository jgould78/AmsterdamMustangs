Mustangs beat Heerenveen at home.

14-Dec-2024

AMSTERDAM, The Netherlands -- The Amsterdam Mustangs defeated Heerenveen at home with a decisive score of 11-5.

The Mustangs exhibited a potent offense, scoring 11 goals with notable performances from Jon Garrity with 2 goals and an assist, Jeremie Gould and Jim Conboy each with a goal and 2 assists, as well as Daniel Watts and Petteri Hyytiäinen each with 3 assists. The score sheet was well-distributed as 10 different Mustangs scored goals, and 16 Mustangs got points. Heerenveen struggled defensively, allowing multiple goals in each period. Both teams maintained a clean game with few penalties called.

The game started with back-and-forth scoring with Heerenveen leading 2-1 halfway into the 1st period. The Mustangs then bounced back with 4 unanswered goals and never looked back. The Mustangs were leading 7-3 after 2 periods. Heerenveen started the 3rd period aggressively with 2 consecutive goals but then the Mustangs managed to score another 4 unanswered to close out the game with an 11-5 win.

This win propels the Mustangs to sole possession of third place in the North division. The Mustangs travel to Leiden on December 22nd where they hope to sweep the season series with the Gladiators.
