Mustangs beat down on Cougars in Hoorn.

09-Nov-2024

HOORN, The Netherlands -- The Amsterdam Mustangs delivered a dominant performance on the road, crushing the Hoorn Cougars with an 11-1 victory. The Mustangs dominated throughout the game, showcasing their offensive prowess and stifling defense.

The Mustangs' offensive firepower was on full display. Jeremie Gould and Jared Penner contributed each with two goals. Petteri Hyytiäinen stood out with three assists and a powerplay goal, demonstrating the team's playmaking ability. Other notable performances came from Krisztian Vizsy with 3 assists and Duane Mattos with a goal and an assist. All lines contributed on the scoresheet versus the Cougars, highlighting the Mustangs’ roster depth.

Mustangs’ goalie Tori Holmes-Kirk had a strong performance as she only conceded one goal. While the Mustangs only had two power play opportunities, they capitalized on one, demonstrating their ability to convert in man-advantage situations. The penalty kill unit was also effective, preventing Hoorn from scoring on any of their power plays.

The resounding victory over Hoorn puts the Mustangs in a good position moving forward. However, the team must maintain their focus and intensity as they prepare for the upcoming challenge on November 24th against the Leiden Gladiators. This game is important as both teams are vying for position while currently sitting in the middle of the 4th division standings.
